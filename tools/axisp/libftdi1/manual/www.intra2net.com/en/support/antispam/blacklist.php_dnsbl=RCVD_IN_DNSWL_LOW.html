Further information on DNSWL LOW

DNSWL.org provides a Whitelist of known legitimate email servers to reduce the chances of false positives while spam filtering. They effectively divide the entire Internet up (by IP) into five categories, and provide you with efficient access methods to this data for free. DNSWL Low - Occasional spam occurrences.
