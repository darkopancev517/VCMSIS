Further information on SURBL MALWARE

SURBLs are lists of web sites that have appeared in unsolicited messages. Unlike most blacklists, SURBLs are not lists of message senders.
As of 1 May 2013 malware data are separated from PH into their own malware list MW.


Name SURBL MALWARE

Spamassassin config

urirhssub   URIBL_MW_SURBL      multi.surbl.org.        A   16
